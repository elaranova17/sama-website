Who We Are

Where Swiss precision meets
the richness of Colombia.

South American Metal Alliance was born from a shared belief: that precious metals deserve to be handled with the same care as the cultures that produce them. We work at the intersection of two worlds — founded in Medellín, governed in La Chaux-de-Fonds — bringing together the warmth of Colombia and the precision of Switzerland.

Our Story

An alliance built
with intention.

South American Metal Alliance came together through years of trust, not opportunity. Our partners chose to build something that would endure — an institution rooted in the mineral wealth of Colombia and guided by the precision of Switzerland.

From the mining regions of Colombia to the financial centres of Europe, we have built relationships that go beyond transactions. For us, every partnership begins with a conversation and is sustained by results.

Our Framework

Ethical practice isn't something we apply at the end of a process — it's built into how we work from the start. Our regulatory framework covers both Colombia and Switzerland, and we hold ourselves to the highest standard of both.

RUCOM

Registro Único de Comercializadores de Minerales. Every transaction is registered with Colombia's National Mining Agency, providing a clear and verifiable record.

ANM · Colombia
